Reflection.

Week 4 introduced us to what an element of chance can look like in both analog and digital formats. The activity prompted us to work with a partner in class to determine a set of parameters that can influence how a game is played. Our game employed two dice which would influence the size, colour, shape, and position of a shape on the page.
